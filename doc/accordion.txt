## Accordion

The following example shows how to wrap elements inside an Accordion
widget.

    #fsharp
    [<JavaScript>]
    let Accordion () =
        let accElems =
            [
                "Panel 1", H1 [Text "Panel 1"]
                "Panel 2", H1 [Text "Panel 2"]
                "Panel 3", H1 [Text "Panel 3"]
            ]
        let accordion = Accordion.New(accElems)
        Div [accordion]
